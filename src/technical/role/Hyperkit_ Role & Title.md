## **1\. Chief Product & Operations Officer (CPOO) / Product Lead (Justine)**

What You Do:

* Define and guide product features, design, user experience, and business logic.  
* Write, deploy, and audit smart contracts for all blockchain components.  
* Create wireframes, design themes, and drive the look/feel of the platform (collaborating with frontend).  
* Manage community channels (Twitter, Telegram, QuestN, Zealy, emails) and maintain project governance (wallets, decision record).  
* Document proposals, technical specs, and user guides for hackathons, investors, and users.  
* Coordinate with external partners (Metis, grant programs).  
* Oversee and help execute product plans and operational strategy.  
* Integrate prompt engineering and AI agent instructions into development and audit flows.  
* Support frontend development by providing design, requirements, and implementation direction.
* Designs backend architecture, APIs, smart contract integrations, and overall system logic.  

His Impact:

* You ensure the product gets built, works, and is well-documented—while keeping the team and user community aligned.  
* You’re the bridge between code, design, docs, and users.

## **2\. Chief Marketing & Frontend Officer (CMFO) / Product Evangelist (Tristan)**

What Tristan Does:

* Builds and codes the UI and frontend systems.  
* Translates wireframes/designs into interactive user interfaces.  
* Delivers public pitches, demos, and presentations at hackathons or investor meetings.  
* Leads marketing campaigns, social outreach, user onboarding, and amplifies project visibility.  
* Crafts pitch decks, demo scripts, and brand messages.  
* Works closely with you (Justine) for design direction.  
* Engages new users, partners, and investors—often as the “public face” of Hyperkit.

His Impact:

* Tristan ensures Hyperkit is easy to use, visually appealing, and reaches new audiences.  
* He turns technical concepts and backend logic into compelling interactive experiences.

## **3\. Chief Technology Officer (CTO) / Project Architect (Aaron)**

What Aaron Does:

* Originates the project idea and technical vision.  
* Designs backend architecture, APIs, smart contract integrations, and overall system logic.  
* Audits all major code (security, correctness, performance), sets code standards.  
* Oversees platform reliability, scalability, and technical decision-making.  
* Leads major engineering efforts, solves complex technical problems.  
* Guides future technical direction, ensures project adapts to new trends and requirements.

His Impact:

* Aaron’s work keeps Hyperkit secure, scalable, and on the right technical path.  
* He ensures all code meets the highest standards and supports long-term growth.
